Once upon a time there were three dragons living together in a big cave. They were siblings. Humphrey was the oldest dragon. He was often cranky but fair-minded. There was Persephone who enjoyed gardening, a difficult task for a dragon and window shopping, also a difficult task for a dragon. And lastly was Shoe. Shoe was the youngest and most rambunctious dragon. He felt is was the task of every dragon to terrorize neighboring villages, burn things for no particular reason and fly all over the place. He was also only ten feet long and couldn't fly so much as glide. He was called Shoe because he was only a little bit bigger than Grandpa Ned's left foot, about the size of his foot if he wore a shoe. As you can imagine, the cave was rather cramped.
